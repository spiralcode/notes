Features

  • A registered user can create documents matching his views or ideas, and edit it anytime he/she needs.
  • All documents created are in public domain, when you publish it. Therefore anyone across the world, can view or access the document, by visiting the document's URL.
  • FOE, doesn't keep a back-up of any document you make. Therefore on deleting a document, it is no longer recoverable.
  • The publisher can either show his identity or hide it from the reader.
  • All documents you make are subject to search engine crawling.
